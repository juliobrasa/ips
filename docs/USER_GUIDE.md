# User Guide

## Getting Started

### Registration

1. Visit the marketplace at `https://your-domain.com`
2. Click **Get Started** or **Register**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Check your email for verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Setting Up Your Company Profile

After registration, you need to create a company profile:

1. Go to **Company Profile** in the sidebar
2. Fill in your company information:
   - **Company Name**: Your business or personal name
   - **Legal Name**: Official registered name (if different)
   - **Tax ID**: NIF, CIF, VAT number, etc.
   - **Country**: Your country of operation
   - **Address**: Full business address
   - **Company Type**: Choose one:
     - **Holder**: You own IP addresses to lease out
     - **Lessee**: You want to lease IP addresses
     - **Both**: You do both
   - **Entity Type**: Individual or Company

### KYC Verification

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required to use the platform:

1. Go to **KYC Documents** in the sidebar
2. Complete these steps:

#### Step 1: Download KYC Form
- Click **Download KYC Form (PDF)**
- The form is pre-filled with your company data
- Print the form

#### Step 2: Upload Identity Document
- **For Individuals**: DNI, NIE, or Passport
- **For Companies**: NIF/CIF document
- Select document type
- Enter document number
- Upload a clear scan or photo (PDF, JPG, PNG - max 10MB)

#### Step 3: Upload Signed KYC Form
- Sign and stamp (if applicable) the printed KYC form
- Scan or photograph the signed form
- Upload as PDF (max 10MB)

#### Step 4: Submit for Review
- Click **Submit for Review**
- Our team will review your documents within 1-2 business days
- You'll be notified of the result by email

## For IP Holders

### Adding a Subnet

1. Go to **My Subnets** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add New Subnet**
3. Enter subnet details:
   - **Network Address**: e.g., 185.123.45.0
   - **CIDR**: Network size (e.g., /24 for 256 IPs)
   - **RIR**: RIPE, ARIN, APNIC, etc.
   - **Country**: Where the IPs are registered
   - **Description**: Additional information
   - **Price per IP**: Monthly price in EUR
   - **Minimum Lease**: Minimum months (1-12)

### Verifying Subnet Ownership

After adding a subnet, you must verify ownership:

1. Go to the subnet detail page
2. Click **Verify Ownership**
3. Choose verification method:
   - **Email Verification**: We send an email to the abuse contact from WHOIS
   - Click the link in the verification email
4. Once verified, your subnet becomes available in the marketplace

### Managing Subnets

- **View WHOIS**: See current WHOIS registration data
- **Check Reputation**: Check IP reputation via AbuseIPDB
- **Edit**: Modify pricing or description
- **Delete**: Remove subnet (only if not leased)

### Tracking Earnings

1. Go to **Payouts** in the sidebar
2. View your earning summary:
   - Pending balance
   - Total earned
   - Payout history
3. Request a payout when ready

## For Lessees

### Browsing the Marketplace

1. Go to **Marketplace** in the sidebar
2. Browse available IP ranges
3. Use filters:
   - **CIDR Size**: /24, /23, /22, etc.
   - **Region**: RIPE, ARIN, APNIC
   - **Price Range**: Min-max per IP
4. Click on a subnet for details

### Adding to Cart

1. On a subnet page, select lease duration (months)
2. Click **Add to Cart**
3. Continue browsing or proceed to checkout

### Checkout Process

1. Go to **Cart** in the sidebar
2. Review your items:
   - Adjust lease duration if needed
   - Remove unwanted items
3. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
4. Review order summary
5. Confirm your order
6. Complete payment

### Managing Leases

1. Go to **My Leases** in the sidebar
2. View all your leases:
   - **Active**: Currently running leases
   - **Pending**: Awaiting payment or activation
   - **Expired**: Past leases
3. For each lease you can:
   - **View Details**: See full lease information
   - **Assign ASN**: Add your ASN number
   - **Download LOA**: Get Letter of Authorization
   - **Renew**: Extend the lease
   - **Terminate**: End the lease early

### Letter of Authorization (LOA)

The LOA is your proof of authorization to use the IP range:

1. Go to your lease detail page
2. Click **Generate LOA** or **Download LOA**
3. The LOA includes:
   - Subnet information
   - Lease period
   - Holder authorization
   - Verification QR code
4. Provide this to your upstream provider

### Invoices

1. Go to **Invoices** in the sidebar
2. View all invoices:
   - **Pending**: Awaiting payment
   - **Paid**: Completed payments
   - **Overdue**: Past due date
3. Click **Pay** to complete payment
4. Download PDF for your records

## Security Tips

1. **Use a strong password**: Minimum 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols
2. **Keep your email secure**: Your account uses email for verification
3. **Review your leases regularly**: Check for unauthorized changes
4. **Download LOAs**: Keep copies for your records
5. **Update contact information**: Ensure we can reach you for important notifications
